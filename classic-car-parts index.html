Who We Are
Passion for Classic Automobiles
We are dedicated enthusiasts and professionals committed to preserving automotive history by providing premium quality classic car parts.

More Than Just Parts — We Preserve History

Founded by lifelong car enthusiasts, Automobile Classic Car Parts has been the trusted name for restorers, collectors, and mechanics seeking hard-to-find components for vintage and classic vehicles.

We carry an extensive inventory spanning decades of automotive excellence — from American muscle cars to European classics — all carefully inspected for quality and authenticity.

  • Genuine OEM and high-quality reproduction parts
  • Knowledgeable staff with decades of expertise
  • Nationwide shipping with secure packaging
  • All parts inspected and quality-guaranteed
  • Special orders and sourcing available
